About CodeQuest

CodeQuest is the flagship competitive programming event organized by the IEEE UCSC Student Branch. This premier coding competition brings together the brightest minds in software development to tackle challenging algorithmic problems.

Whether you're a seasoned programmer or just starting your coding journey, CodeQuest offers an opportunity to test your skills, learn from peers, and compete for exciting prizes while building lasting connections in the tech community.

Frequently Asked Questions

What programming languages are allowed?

You can use any programming language including C++, Java, Python, JavaScript, C#, and more. Choose the language you're most comfortable with.

What should I bring to the event?

Bring your laptop, chargers, student ID, and enthusiasm! We'll provide WiFi, power outlets, meals, and refreshments throughout the 24-hour period.

Is there any preparation material available?

Yes! We provide free workshop sessions, practice problems, and algorithm tutorials leading up to the event. Check our website for updated resources.

What is the registration fee for CodeQuest 2025?

Registration is completely free for all university students. We believe in making competitive programming accessible to everyone regardless of financial background.

Can I participate individually or do I need a team?

You can participate both individually or as part of a team (maximum 3 members). Team categories have separate prizes and individual participants can also win exciting rewards.
